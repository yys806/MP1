<section markdown="1">

## Problem

<ul>
<li>Mixed-autonomy traffic generation must model human-driven and autonomous vehicles under uncertainty, heterogeneity, and safety-critical interaction.</li>
<li>Offline trajectory sampling alone is not enough for evaluating future traffic systems because generated behavior must remain executable in closed-loop dynamics.</li>
</ul>

</section>
<section markdown="1">

## Method

<ul>
<li>Heterogeneity-aware conditional encoding for traffic context and agent states.</li>
<li>Conditional diffusion trajectory generation guided by imitation priors.</li>
<li>Risk-aware long-tail feedback and progressive adversarial alignment for closed-loop correction.</li>
</ul>

</section>
<section markdown="1">

## My Role

<ul>
<li>First author; responsible for problem formulation, method design, implementation, experiments, figure preparation, and manuscript writing.</li>
<li>Worked under the guidance of Prof. Minghui Liwang at Tongji University.</li>
</ul>
