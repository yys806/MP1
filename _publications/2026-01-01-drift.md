**Yaoshen Yu**, Minghui Liwang, Wenbo Zhu, Xinlei Yi, Yiguang Hong, Yuhan Su, and Seyyedali Hosseinalipour. *DRIFT: Risk-Constrained Diffusion with Imitation Priors for Mixed-Autonomy Traffic Generation*. Manuscript in preparation for submission to *IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems*.

DRIFT studies traffic generation for future mixed-autonomy systems where human-driven and autonomous vehicles interact under uncertainty, heterogeneity, and safety-critical coupling. The work frames trajectory generation as a closed-loop problem rather than an offline sampling task: behaviors are generated, filtered, selected, and validated under executable traffic dynamics.

The method unifies heterogeneity-aware conditional encoding, conditional diffusion-based trajectory generation, imitation priors, and progressive adversarial alignment with risk-aware long-tail feedback. The evaluation is designed around safety, efficiency, and closed-loop stability across multiple scenarios and autonomous-vehicle penetration regimes.

**禹尧珅**、李王明卉、朱文博、易鑫磊、洪一光、苏宇涵、Seyyedali Hosseinalipour。*DRIFT: Risk-Constrained Diffusion with Imitation Priors for Mixed-Autonomy Traffic Generation*。论文拟投稿 *IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems*。

DRIFT 面向未来人类驾驶车辆与自动驾驶车辆混行的交通系统，关注异质性、不确定性与安全约束下的轨迹生成问题。它不是把生成结果停留在离线采样层面，而是把生成、筛选、选择与闭环验证统一到可执行交通动力学流程中。

方法上，DRIFT 结合异质性感知条件编码、条件扩散轨迹生成、模仿先验，以及带风险感知长尾反馈的渐进式对抗对齐。实验从安全性、效率与闭环稳定性三个维度评估模型在不同场景和自动驾驶渗透率下的表现。
