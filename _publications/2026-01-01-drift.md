**Yaoshen Yu**, [Minghui Liwang](https://faculty.tongji.edu.cn/liwangminghui/zh_CN/index.htm), Wenbo Zhu, Xinlei Yi, Yiguang Hong, Yuhan Su, and Seyyedali Hosseinalipour. *DRIFT: Risk-Constrained Diffusion with Imitation Priors for Mixed-Autonomy Traffic Generation*. arXiv preprint [arXiv:2606.16589](http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16589).

DRIFT studies traffic generation for future mixed-autonomy systems where human-driven vehicles and autonomous vehicles interact under uncertainty, heterogeneity, and safety-critical coupling. Instead of treating generation as an offline sampling problem, the work frames it as a closed-loop pipeline in which trajectories are generated, filtered, selected, and validated through executable traffic dynamics.

The method integrates heterogeneity-aware conditional encoding, conditional diffusion-based trajectory generation, imitation priors, and progressive adversarial alignment with risk-aware long-tail feedback. The evaluation focuses on safety, efficiency, and closed-loop stability across multiple traffic scenarios and autonomous-vehicle penetration regimes.

**禹尧珅**、[李王明卉](https://faculty.tongji.edu.cn/liwangminghui/zh_CN/index.htm)、朱文博、易鑫磊、洪一光、苏宇涵、Seyyedali Hosseinalipour。*DRIFT: Risk-Constrained Diffusion with Imitation Priors for Mixed-Autonomy Traffic Generation*。论文已发布为 arXiv 预印本 [arXiv:2606.16589](http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16589)。

DRIFT 面向未来人类驾驶车辆与自动驾驶车辆混行的交通系统，关注不确定性、异质性与安全关键耦合下的交通轨迹生成问题。该工作不把生成停留在离线采样层面，而是将轨迹生成、筛选、选择与验证统一到可执行交通动力学闭环中。

方法上，DRIFT 结合异质性感知条件编码、条件扩散轨迹生成、模仿先验，以及带有风险感知长尾反馈的渐进式对抗对齐。实验评估围绕安全性、效率与闭环稳定性展开，覆盖多类交通场景和不同自动驾驶渗透率设置。
