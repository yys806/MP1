**Module:** Campus Projects

This Shanghai Municipal University Student Innovation and Entrepreneurship Project explored passenger-flow sensing and optimized carriage allocation for subway systems. The project combined transportation problems with computing methods, using sensing data and lightweight analysis to reason about crowdedness and allocation strategies.

The work emphasized an applied engineering path: understand the station and carriage scenarios, collect or process passenger-flow signals, and translate them into decisions that could improve carriage distribution and passenger experience. It sits at the intersection of urban mobility, sensing, and optimization.

**Keywords:** subway passenger flow, sensing, optimization, Raspberry Pi, computer vision

**模块：** 校内项目

这是上海市大学生创新创业训练计划项目，围绕地铁客流感知与车厢分配优化展开。项目把交通场景问题和计算方法结合起来，通过客流感知、轻量分析和优化策略，探索如何改善车厢拥挤与乘客分布。

项目重点在于真实应用路径：理解站台与车厢场景，采集或处理客流信号，再把数据转化为可执行的分配建议。它连接了城市交通、传感技术与优化决策。

**关键词：** 地铁客流，感知，优化，树莓派，计算机视觉
