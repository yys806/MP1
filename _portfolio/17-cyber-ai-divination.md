**Module:** Web Projects

Cyber AI Divination is an AI fortune-simulation and psychological-consultation assistant. The application was generated from the Google Gemini AI Studio template and extended as a TypeScript/React web app with serverless functions and service modules. It presents divination-style interactions such as fortune analysis while keeping the implementation deployable as a modern web application.

**模块：** 网页项目
