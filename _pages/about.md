# Yaoshen Yu

I am Yaoshen Yu, an undergraduate student majoring in Artificial Intelligence at Tongji University. My work sits at the intersection of mixed-autonomy traffic generation, vision-language model evaluation, network-state world models, embedded systems, and product-oriented software engineering.

## Education

- **Tongji University**, B.Eng. in Artificial Intelligence, 2023 - Present

## Research and Experience

- **DRIFT: Risk-Constrained Diffusion with Imitation Priors for Mixed-Autonomy Traffic Generation**, first author, 2025.11 - Present. Available as [arXiv:2606.16589](http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16589). I am working on this paper under the guidance of [Prof. Minghui Liwang](https://faculty.tongji.edu.cn/liwangminghui/zh_CN/index.htm) at Tongji University.
- **[PCNI Group](http://pcni.pku.edu.cn/homepage.html), School of Electronics, Peking University**, research intern, 2026.03 - Present. Work on network-state world models and action-conditioned prediction under the supervision of [Prof. Jianan Zhang](https://ele.pku.edu.cn/info/1023/2493.htm) ([personal homepage](https://jianan-z.github.io/)).
- **[Prof. Xinlei Chen's Group](https://www.sigs.tsinghua.edu.cn/cxl/main.psp), Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School**, research intern, 2026.03 - Present. Work on traffic-scene VLM evaluation, visual bias analysis, and counterfactual diagnostics.
- **Tongji University Quality Control Office**, developer, 2025.07 - 2026.05. Built agent-assisted workflows for quality-data management and analysis.

## Interests

Outside research and engineering, I enjoy [cubing](https://yys806.github.io/2026/02/05/%E9%AD%94%E6%96%B9%E5%85%AC%E5%BC%8F%E6%80%BB%E7%BB%93), running, [films](https://shen806.de5.net/), and building small tools that make daily life slightly more elegant.
